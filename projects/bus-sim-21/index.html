bus simulator 21

Bus Simulator 21 is the latest installment in the popular bus simulation series. On this project, I had the opportunity to contribute to a game that allows players to build and manage their own bus company while driving a wide variety of officially licensed buses in two expansive, open-world maps. During the "Next Stop" update, we focused on improving the overall gameplay and providing live support, as well as creating memorable DLCs.

role & project details

  • Genre: Simulator
  • Team Size: 30+
  • Engine: Unreal Engine 4
  • Duration: 1 year +
